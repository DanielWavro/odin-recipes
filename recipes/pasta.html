The Perfect Pasta!

What you will need:

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 teapsoons italian seasoning
  • 10 ounces of penne pasta
  • 1 jar tomato sauce

The Steps:

  1. In a skillet, fry the bacon until crispy
  2. Take out bacon and add chicken to same skillet. Season with italian seasoning mix
  3. Boil water, then cook the pene pasta as directed
  4. Dice the bacon and chicken, then in the same skillet cook together with the tomato sauce
  5. Enjoy!

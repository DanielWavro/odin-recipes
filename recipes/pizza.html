The Perfect Homemade Pizza!

What you will need:

  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella

The Steps:

  1. Mix flour, salt, and yeast
  2. Add water and oil, then knead mixture for 2 minutes and cover in bowl
  3. Take out and pat into circle
  4. Paste on tomato sauce, then sprinkle mozzarella cheese
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
